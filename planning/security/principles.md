# Principles

- Require explicit trust boundaries, auth rules, and data-handling expectations before approving work.
- Prefer server-enforced or OS-enforced controls over UI promises.
- Ask for auditability when sensitive data leaves its normal boundary or when privileged bulk actions are introduced.
- Keep questions concrete and proportional so the team can unblock the plan rather than drown in hypothetical attacks.
- When another role proposes a shortcut that changes risk, explain the smallest safe shape instead of simply saying no.
