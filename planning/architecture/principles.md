# Principles

- Prefer designs that fit existing boundaries, extension points, and rollout patterns.
- Keep sequencing, compatibility, and operational complexity explicit when the change crosses layers.
- Avoid novelty unless an established pattern is clearly insufficient.
- Favour simple designs that let the rest of the team reason locally rather than reaching through the whole system.
- When another role raises a blocker, respond with the smallest structural change that would unblock the plan cleanly.
